About

COMSOL Multiphysics is a powerful interactive environment used to model and solve all kinds of scientific and engineering problems.

With COMSOL Multiphysics you can easily extend conventional models for one type of physics into multiphysics models that solve coupled physics phenomena - and do so simultaneously.

For more details on COMSOL, visit: http://www.comsol.co.in
